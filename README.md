# TENZING Japan Expansion Strategy

International Marketing project completed as part of the MSc Marketing program at KEDGE Business School.

This project explores the international expansion strategy of TENZING, a premium UK-based natural energy drink brand, entering the Japanese market.

---

  Project Overview

The report analyzes Japan’s growing demand for healthy and functional beverages and evaluates how TENZING can successfully position itself as a premium natural energy drink in the market.

The project combines international marketing frameworks, market research, consumer insights, and strategic recommendations tailored to Japanese consumers.

---

 Objectives

* Analyze the Japanese energy drink market
* Identify consumer behavior and health trends
* Evaluate market opportunities and competition
* Develop an international market entry strategy
* Create STP (Segmentation, Targeting & Positioning)
* Recommend marketing mix adaptations for Japan
* Explore sustainability and CSR initiatives

---

---

 Key Strategic Recommendations

* Position TENZING as a **premium natural energy drink**
* Focus on **plant-based and low-sugar ingredients**
* Launch Japan-specific flavors such as **Matcha Mint & Yuzu**
* Use sustainable and recyclable packaging
* Target Millennials & Gen Z consumers
* Leverage Instagram, LINE, TikTok, and influencer marketing
